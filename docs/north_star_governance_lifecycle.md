---

## Governance Outcomes

ALLOW

Execution may proceed.

DENY

Execution may not proceed.

RESTRICT

Execution is held or constrained until required governance conditions are satisfied.

---

## RESTRICT Lifecycle

RESTRICT

↓

HOLDING

↓

Required Action

↓

Approval or Additional Evidence

↓

Re-Evaluation

↓

ALLOW or DENY

↓

Execution Status

---

## Canonical Caregiver Example

- Alice is the subject.
- Beth is Alice's caregiver.
- A mission step is requested.
- Authority evidence is present.
- Alice's Subject Agency State is SUPERVISED.
- Governance issues RESTRICT.
- Execution enters HOLDING.
- Beth approval is recorded.
- Governance re-evaluates.
- Execution proceeds after ALLOW.

---

## Design Principle

Mission steps are primary.

Authority evidence is supporting input.

Execution consumes governance decisions.

Execution does not reconstruct governance logic.
