# Discovery: Governed Team Presence
# Date: June 26, 2026

## Discovery

The long-term vision crystallized during the AAuth integration session.

The goal is not to build smarter agents. The goal is to create governed
teams where humans and autonomous participants collaborate safely,
visibly, and continuously under execution-time authority.

## Execution Targets

SOGA remains identical regardless of execution target.

Execution targets may include:
- A2A agents
- MCP tools
- Enterprise APIs
- Robots (Misty)
- Home automation
- Future autonomous systems

## Misty's Role

Misty is not the innovation. Misty provides physical presence for the
team, making previously invisible AI collaboration visible in shared space.

Instead of interacting with isolated chat windows, the team convenes.
Misty becomes the embodied facilitator of governed conversations.

## Emerging Research Questions

- How do humans collaborate with persistent autonomous teammates?
- How should autonomous participants communicate?
- When should an agent interrupt?
- When should an agent remain silent?
- How is authority expressed socially?
- How is runtime governance reflected in group behavior?

## Research Area

Governed Team Presence extends beyond AI governance, robotics, identity,
and authorization into human-computer interaction and collaborative
systems research.

## Funding Implications

This vision supports NSF Human-Centered Computing framing.
The research question becomes testable with Misty as a physical presence.
